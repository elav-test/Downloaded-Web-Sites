Storytelling for Lawyers
Alice’s Adventures on Wonderland was published in 1865. Some stories are unforgettable but why?

Storytelling for Lawyers unlocks the secret of how to tell exceptional stories.

  • Explore the rapidly evolving world of story science
  • Discover the universal code underlying memorable stories
  • Learn how to persuade people with compelling stories
A science-based journey into the world of storytelling

Our Services

I. Workshop
II. Facilitator

Storytelling for Lawyers helps you tell powerfully persuasive factual stories. You will explore the latest scientific research to understand why people tell stories, decode Alice’s Adventures in Wonderland to discover the universal source code of story and acquire a practical storytelling toolkit. You will learn how to:

  • Structure stories and create characters
  • Transport audiences into the world of your story
  • Demonstrate cognitive and affective empathy

The day includes a live psychology experiment, classic movie clips and a storyboard exercise.
